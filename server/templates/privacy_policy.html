Privacy Policy

How the She Wants You Telegram bot (“the Bot”) handles your information.

1. What we store

To run the Bot we keep only what is needed for chat, memory, and credits:

  • Telegram user ID — account identification and credit balance
  • Message credits — your remaining paid and free messages
  • Chat content — recent messages and a long-term memory summary so the Bot can continue your conversation
  • Payment reference — Stripe payment ID and credited amount (not card numbers)

We do not collect email addresses, phone numbers, or billing details on our servers. Card data is handled entirely by Stripe.

2. How your data is protected

  • Data is stored in secured PostgreSQL and Redis databases with access limited to the service
  • Your messages and memory are kept to provide the Bot — not for advertising or resale
  • The development team does not use your chats or personal data for marketing, analytics, model training, or any purpose outside operating the Bot

3. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties for their own purposes. Your conversation history is not handed over to advertisers, data brokers, or unrelated services.

The only external processing required to run the Bot is: Stripe (secure payment processing) and our AI provider (your message is sent only to generate a reply for you in that session). We do not pass your data on for anyone else’s use.

4. Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. We never store:

  • Credit card numbers
  • Full payment credentials
  • Billing addresses on our side

5. Deleting your data

In Telegram, send /delete_info and confirm to clear your saved chat memory (recent window and long-term summary). Your Telegram ID and credit balance remain so you can keep using the Bot. For anything else, contact us via /contacts in the Bot.

6. Policy updates

We may update this page from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes means you accept the updated policy.

Refund Policy

Rules for purchases of message credits in the Bot.

1. All sales are final

Paid message packs are non-refundable. Once payment is completed, we do not return money — whether credits were used or not. Please read the pack details in /credits and make sure you want the purchase before you pay.

2. Think before you buy

Credits are digital goods delivered immediately after a successful payment. There is no trial refund after purchase. If you are unsure, use your free messages first and buy only when you are ready to continue.

3. Technical issues

If a payment succeeds but credits are not added because of a technical failure on our side, contact us via /contacts. We will add the missing credits as soon as possible. This is not a cash refund — it is a fix for a failed delivery of what you already paid for.

4. Legal exceptions

Nothing above limits rights you may have under mandatory consumer law in your country, where such law applies.
